Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Sustainability Tracker?

The Sustainability Tracker is an app designed to help users track their environmental impact by measuring their carbon emissions, water usage, and energy consumption.

How can I set sustainability goals?

Once you sign up, you can set personal sustainability goals, such as reducing your carbon footprint, saving water, and minimizing energy consumption.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the app offers a free version with essential tracking features. For advanced features, a premium version is available.
